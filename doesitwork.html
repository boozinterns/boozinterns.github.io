Survey & Model Test Run

Does the predictive model work?

The employee engagement questions were used to survey interns at Booz Allen to calculate their engagement index scores. This index then went into the predictive analytics model to calculate LTCR. Here are the average engagement scores we found:

Supervisor: 80, Intrinsic Work Experience: 79.2, Leaders Lead: 85.2, Satisfaction: 75.2

This gives an overall average engagement score of 79.9, which results in an LTCR of .53 per 100 people for Booz Allen interns. Comparatively, the average LTCR for the Federal Agency survey was .6 cases per 100 people.
